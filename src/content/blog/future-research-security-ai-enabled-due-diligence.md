<section class="intro-section">
<p class="lead">The global research landscape is more interconnected than ever before, but this openness comes with significant risks. As foreign adversaries increasingly target academic and corporate research, <strong>traditional due diligence methods are no longer sufficient</strong>.</p>
<p>Current vetting processes for international collaborators and grant applicants are often manual, labor-intensive, and prone to oversight. To stay ahead of sophisticated adversaries, organizations must embrace the future of research security: <strong>AI-enabled due diligence</strong>.</p>
</section>

<section>
<h2>The Challenge: Scale and Sophistication</h2>
<p>Without proper vetting, academic institutions, government agencies, and corporate R&D teams risk economic espionage and severe reputational damage. The sheer volume of international collaborations makes it nearly impossible for compliance teams to manually identify hidden foreign ties or undisclosed funding sources.</p>
<div class="highlight-box">
<p>This challenge is compounded by the fact that adversaries are becoming increasingly sophisticated in <strong>concealing their affiliations</strong> and exploiting the open nature of academic publishing and collaboration networks.</p>
</div>
</section>

<section>
<h2>The Solution: AI-Powered Vetting</h2>
<p>Tools like Grant Hopper, the world's first AI-enabled due diligence platform, are designed specifically to assess research personnel for potential foreign influence risks.</p>
<div class="factor-grid">
<div class="factor-item">
<div class="factor-icon">🤖</div>
<h4>Instant Analysis</h4>
<p>By analyzing vast amounts of open-source data and cross-referencing global affiliations, AI platforms can quickly identify high-risk collaborations connected to foreign military or defense industrial bases.</p>
</div>
<div class="factor-item">
<div class="factor-icon">📊</div>
<h4>Actionable Insights</h4>
<p>This automated approach provides actionable insights, allowing organizations to make informed decisions swiftly and efficiently, without the need for large teams of analysts.</p>
</div>
<div class="factor-item">
<div class="factor-icon">⚡</div>
<h4>Reduced Burden</h4>
<p>The integration of AI into research security significantly reduces the administrative burden on compliance offices while enhancing accuracy of threat detection.</p>
</div>
</div>
</section>
